This Privacy Policy describes how Mystic Tarot ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our iOS application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

Email address and display name when you register with email/password.

If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive only the basic profile information those services provide (name and email).

Information You Provide

Your zodiac sign and date of birth, stored locally on your device and used solely to personalize horoscope and numerology content.

Usage Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized crash reports and aggregate usage statistics (for example, which features are used most). This data does not personally identify you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Create and manage your account.

Personalize your tarot readings, horoscope, and numerology results.

Send you daily tarot notifications if you enable them (you can disable these at any time in Settings).

Process and manage your in-app subscription through Apple's App Store.

Improve the App through anonymized analytics.

3. Data Storage

Your reading history and preferences are stored locally on your device using the iOS file system.

Your account credentials are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication — we never store passwords.

Your zodiac sign and birth date are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on several third-party services:

Firebase (Google LLC) — authentication and anonymized analytics.

Google Sign-In — optional login method.

Apple Sign In — optional login method.

Apple App Store — subscription billing. Apple processes all payment data, and we never see your payment details.

5. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment card information. Subscription status is verified on the device using Apple's StoreKit framework.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

This permanently removes your Firebase account and authentication data.

Local reading history can be cleared from the Journal tab.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
